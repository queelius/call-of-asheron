The most significant structural issue is the chronological relationship between Volume I (Chapters 10-12) and Volumes II-III. Specifically:
- **Ch10 (Four Paths, One Threshold)** presents the first convergence at ~3 months after arrival
- **Ch14 (The First Convergence)** presents "the first convergence" at ~2 years after arrival
- **Ch12 (The Architect's Gambit)** has Asheron appearing physically and revealing the time differential
- **Ch16 (The Ambush)** also has Asheron appearing physically and revealing the time differential

These may be:
1. The same events told twice (Volume I detailed, Volume II compressed recap) -- LIKELY
2. Different events at different times -- POSSIBLE but confusing
3. A chronological restructuring where Volume I Chapters 10-12 are set later than their position implies
